Cinematic

UX / UI · Web & Mobile Platform · 2024

UX Research User Flows UI Design Prototyping E-commerce

Context

Cinematic is a centralized platform that brings together all movie theaters in the country—regardless of brand—into a single ecosystem. Users can browse movies, compare theaters, purchase tickets, and order candy from one unified app or website.

Problem

  • Movie theaters operate in isolated ecosystems
  • Users must navigate multiple apps and websites
  • Inconsistent UX across different cinema brands
  • No centralized ticket + candy purchasing experience

Goal

Design a centralized cinema platform that simplifies movie discovery, ticket purchasing, and candy ordering—while remaining profitable through a minimal 2% service fee per transaction.

Process

Main approach: Market analysis → user research → user flows → wireframing → UI design → prototyping → validation.

Wireframes & User Flow

The core challenge was reducing friction in the ticket buying process while supporting multiple cinema brands and seating systems.

Key Screens

  • 65% of users abandoned ticket purchases due to complex flows
  • Unified flow reduced the process to 3 main steps
  • Goal: Increase completed purchases while maintaining a seamless experience across different cinema brands

Final UI Design

The final UI focuses on clarity, brand neutrality, and cinematic atmosphere, allowing different theaters to coexist under a single visual system.

Business Model

Cinematic operates with a minimal 2% service fee per transaction, making it attractive for theaters while sustaining the platform.

  • No exclusivity required for cinemas
  • Lower fees than traditional ticketing platforms
  • Additional revenue through featured placements

Value Proposition

A single platform for users, a low-cost distribution channel for cinemas, and a scalable centralized system for the movie industry.

Results

  • ✔ Simplified multi-cinema ticket purchasing
  • ✔ Increased completion rate in checkout flow
  • ✔ Seamless integration of candy and ticket sales
