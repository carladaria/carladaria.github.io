I'm a multimedia artist shaped by the worlds I've built digital, painted, coded, and imagined.
My path began in the least artistic of places: high-school chemistry. While formulas escaped me, technical drawing revealed where my mind truly belonged: in images, structure, and storytelling.

That instinct led me from traditional painting to the National University of the Arts, where I studied Multimedia Arts and entered the gaming world professionally. I later expanded into web programming, UX/UI design, and project management through Google's professional programs, blending creativity with structure and intuition with technique.
Today, I work as a 3D artist at Fanny Pack Studios and as an audiovisual developer for independent and freelance projects, bringing together art, technology, and play.

Even with years of experience, I consider myself a lifelong learner; always expanding my skills and perspectives to stay connected to an ever-evolving creative world.
